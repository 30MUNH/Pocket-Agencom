Marketing Playbook

Strategic overview and core messaging pillars.

Core Objective

To drive a 25% increase in online sales during the first two weeks of the Spring Collection launch by leveraging scarcity, exclusive access, and high-quality educational content across social channels.

Target Audience Persona

"The Mindful Millennial" - Ages 25-35. Values sustainability, quality over quantity, and aesthetics. Active primarily on Instagram and TikTok. Responsive to authentic brand storytelling and transparency.

Key Messaging Pillars

1. Sustainable Quality Focusing on materials that last and ethical production methods.
2. Effortless Versatility Highlighting how pieces mix and match for everyday wear.

Launch Timeline Highlights

  • Week -1 (Tease): Blurred sneak peeks, countdowns on stories, email list builder campaigns.
  • Week 0 (Launch): VIP early access email, high-production launch video, influencer unboxings.
  • Week 1 (Sustain): User-generated content sharing, deep-dives into individual products.

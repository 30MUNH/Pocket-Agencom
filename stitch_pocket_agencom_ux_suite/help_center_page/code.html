forum Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my billing information?

expand_more

You can update your billing details by navigating to Settings > Billing. From there, click on "Edit Payment Method" to securely enter your new card information.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

expand_more

Absolutely. You can change your subscription tier at any time. Any changes will be pro-rated for your current billing cycle. Just visit My Plans to see upgrade options.

Where can I find the Marketing Kit templates?

expand_more

All active templates are stored in your Template Library. You can search by campaign type, platform, or industry to find exactly what you need quickly.

How do I invite team members?

expand_more

To add a team member, go to Settings > Team and click "Invite User". Enter their email address and assign them a role. They will receive an email with instructions to join your workspace.
